# Part 1: Beta Program

## Beta Objectives

| Objective | Target |
|-----------|--------|
| Partners onboarded | 5 |
| Active daily users | 5 |
| Cases created | 15+ |
| Documents uploaded | 50+ |
| Feedback items | 20+ |
| Testimonials | 3+ |

## Beta Timeline (Weeks 1-4)

### Week 1: Selection (March 26 – April 1)

**Day 1-2:** Identify candidates
- Reach out to 5 partners you know best
- Select those who are vocal, active, and early adopters
- Avoid: partners who complain a lot or are always busy

**Day 3-4:** Send invitation
- Personal message (not mass email)
- Explain beta: free in exchange for feedback
- Include: what to expect, time commitment (2 hrs/week)

**Day 5-7:** Confirm and schedule
- Get 3-5 verbal commitments
- Schedule onboarding calls for Week 2

### Week 2: Onboarding (April 2-8)

**Day 1 (Monday):** Send welcome package
- Welcome email with login credentials
- Getting Started guide (PDF)
- Link to schedule onboarding call

**Day 2-3 (Tue-Wed):** Onboarding calls (15 min each)
- Call 1: Walk through dashboard + creating first client
- Call 2: Upload first document + create first case
- Record calls (with permission) for training videos

**Day 4-7 (Thu-Sun):** Self-guided practice
- Partners explore Phase 1 features
- Encourage: Add at least 2 clients, 5 documents, 3 cases

### Week 3: Active Usage (April 9-15)

**Daily:** Monitor usage
- Check who logged in
- Note who created cases/uploaded docs
- Identify blockers early

**Mid-week:** First feedback check-in
- Slack/Discord message: "How's it going?"
- Collect: What's working? What's frustrating?
- Document in shared feedback spreadsheet

**Weekend:** No pressure — let them use naturally

### Week 4: Refinement (April 16-22)

**Collect final feedback:**
- What feature would you miss most if Orvared disappeared?
- What would make you recommend it to another advocate?
- Any specific pain points we should fix before launch?

**Document testimonials:**
- Ask 3 advocates for quotes
- Get permission for name + photo (or anonymous)
- Capture specific use cases

# Part 2: Onboarding

## The "5-Minute Win" Onboarding Flow

This onboarding is designed to get users to their first "win" in 5 minutes or less.

### Step 1: Welcome Email (Automated, Day 0)

**Subject:** Welcome to Orvared — Let's get started

**Body:**
> Hi [Name],
>
> Welcome to Orvared! You're about to discover the only full-stack platform for special education advocates and evaluators.
>
> 🚀 **Your login:** [link]
> 🔑 **Password:** [set by user]
>
> **Here's your 5-minute quickstart:**
> 1. Create your first client profile (3 min)
> 2. Upload one document (1 min)
> 3. Create your first case (1 min)
>
> → Watch the 2-minute video: [link]
>
> Questions? Reply to this email or ping us in #support.
>
> Let's do this,
> The Orvared Team

### Step 2: In-App Guided Walkthrough (Day 0-1)

When they first log in, show tooltips in this order:

| Step | Tooltip Message | Location |
|------|----------------|----------|
| 1 | "Click here to add your first client" | Clients page |
| 2 | "Add their name and contact info" | New client form |
| 3 | "Great! Now let's add a document" | Documents tab |
| 4 | "Upload any IEP, eval, or report" | Upload button |
| 5 | "Now create a case to organize everything" | Cases page |

>
> → Watch the full tutorial: [link]

### Step 4: Day 7 Check-In

**Subject:** How's your first week with Orvared?

**Body:**

| Resource | Where to Find |
|----------|---------------|
| Getting Started Guide | Welcome email + in-app help |
| Video Tutorials | In-app help center |

### Tier 2: Community (All Users)

- Private Discord/Slack channel
- Search past threads
- Peer-to-peer help

### Tier 3: Direct Support (Paid Users)

- Email: support@orvared.com
- Response time: 24 hours
- In-app chat (within dashboard)

- Dedicated Slack channel
- Phone availability
- 4-hour response SLA
