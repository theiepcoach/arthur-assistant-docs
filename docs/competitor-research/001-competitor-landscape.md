# Competitor Research Update

**Date:** 2026-03-26

## New Competitors Found

### AdvocateIQ (NEW - March 2026)
- **Launch:** March 21, 2026
- **Target:** Parents in Texas initially
- **Offer:** Professional-grade document review + advocacy support
- **Orvared risk:** Low (parents vs. professionals), but expansion likely

### Playground IEP
- **Focus:** AI tools for IEP writing, goal generation, progress monitoring
- **Target:** Schools/special educators
- **Orvared differentiation:** Still school-focused, not advocate-focused

### EmbraceIEP
- **Focus:** Web-based IEP compliance, timeline tracking
- **Target:** Districts/schools
- **Orvared differentiation:** District-focused, not independent advocates

## Competitive Landscape Summary

**Orvared's position remains unique:**
- No direct "advocate/evaluator operating system" competitor
- AdvocateIQ is parent-focused (potential future competitor)
- Playground IEP is school-focused
- Market gap: Independent advocates & evaluators still underserved
