# Orvared Onboarding

This is the updated onboarding document for Orvared.

## Step 1: Landing Page

The landing page shows the following information:

* Hero tagline: "The O That Organizes Special Education"
* Timeline view showing upcoming and completed events
* Alerts

## Step 2: Sign Up & Plan Selection

The sign up page shows the following information:

* Plan selection (two options):
  - **Advocate**: $129/mo, 1 seat, 10 active students
  - **Evaluator**: $79/mo, 1 seat, 10 active students
* Form fields: full name, advocacy firm name, email address, password
* Teal button: "Sign Up & Pay — $129/mo →"
* Link: "Already have an account? Sign in"

## Step 3: Checkout (Stripe)

The checkout page shows the following information:

* Order summary: service, price, and payment options
* Payment options: card, Apple Pay, Cash App Pay, Klarna, bank transfer
* Email pre-filled: coach_d@me.com

## Step 4: Post-Payment Confirmation

The post-payment confirmation page shows the following information:

* Success message: "Your payment was successful!"
* Button: "Sign In"

## Step 5: Sign In

The sign in page shows the following information:

* Welcome back screen
* Email pre-filled: coach_d@me.com
* Password field
* "Sign In" button
* Option: Continue with Google
* Link: "Don't have an account? Create one free"

## What's Next? (Recommended Setup)

After reaching the dashboard, complete the following steps:

1. Add a Contact → CRM → Add Contact
2. Convert to Client → CRM → [Contact] → Convert to Client
3. Add a Student → Students → Add Student
4. Upload First Document → Student → Documents tab
5. Set Your Hourly Rate → Settings → Firm Info

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Payment fails | Check card details, try alternate payment method |
| Can't sign in | Use password reset or continue with Google |
| Dashboard shows zeros | Add contacts, clients, and students to populate |
| Need to change plan | Contact support or upgrade through billing |

*For support: Use the chat bubble in the bottom right corner*
