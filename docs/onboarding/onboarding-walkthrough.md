## What's Next? (Recommended Setup)

After reaching the dashboard, complete these steps:

1. **Add a Contact** → CRM → Add Contact
2. **Convert to Client** → CRM → [Contact] → Convert to Client
3. **Add a Student** → Students → Add Student
4. **Upload First Document** → Student → Documents tab
5. **Set Your Hourly Rate** → Settings → Firm Info

---

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Payment fails | Check card details, try alternate payment method |
| Can't sign in | Use password reset or continue with Google |
| Dashboard shows zeros | Add contacts, clients, and students to populate |
| Need to change plan | Contact support or upgrade through billing |
