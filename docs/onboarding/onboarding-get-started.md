# Get Started with Orvared
## Your Post-Signup Implementation Guide

---

## Welcome to Orvared! 🎉

This guide walks you through setting up your special education advocacy practice management system. Complete these steps to get the most out of Orvared.

**Estimated setup time:** 30-45 minutes

---

## Step 1: Configure Your Firm Settings

**Where:** Settings → Firm Info

**Do this first!** Your firm settings apply to everything.

### Required:
- **Firm/Organization Name** - This appears on invoices
- **Business Address** - For invoice billing address
- **Default Hourly Rate** - Used when generating invoices (you can override per-case)
- **Contact Email** - For client communications

### Recommended:
- **Logo** - Upload your firm logo for professional invoices
- **Default Invoice Terms** - Net 30, Net 15, etc.
- **Time Zone** - Ensures deadlines display correctly

---

## Step 2: Add Your Contacts (CRM)

**Where:** CRM → Add Contact

Before you can create cases or students, you need contacts.

### What to add:
- **Leads** - Potential clients who haven't signed up yet
- **Current Clients** - People you're actively serving
- **Professional Contacts** - School district contacts, evaluators, attorneys

### For each contact, add:
- Name (first/last or organization name)
- Email
- Phone
- Address
- Notes

**Pro tip:** Add everyone you might work with now. You can convert leads to clients later.

---

## Step 3: Convert Contact to Client

**Where:** CRM → [Contact Name] → "Convert to Client"

When you're ready to start working with someone, convert them from a lead to a client.

### What happens:
- Creates a Client record linked to the Contact
- Keeps the relationship so you see their full history
- Client can now access the portal (if enabled)

---

## Step 4: Create Student Records

**Where:** Students → Add Student

Each case needs a student record (the child receiving services).

### Required for each student:
- **First Name** and **Last Name**
- **Date of Birth** - Determines grade level, eligibility timelines
- **Parent/Guardian** - Link to the Client record
- **School District** - For compliance tracking
- **Primary Disability** (optional but recommended)

### Optional but helpful:
- Grade level
- IEP meeting dates
- Notes about the student

---

## Step 5: Upload Documents

**Where:** Student → Documents Tab OR Case → Documents Tab

Start building your case file.

### What to upload first:
- **IEPs** - Current and prior
- **Evaluations** - Educational, related services
- **Correspondence** - Letters from district
- **Reports** - Any assessments or progress reports

### Supported formats:
- PDF (recommended)
- DOC/DOCX
- Images (PNG, JPG)
- Audio files for transcription

### How it works:
1. Click "Upload Document"
2. Select file from computer
3. Choose document type
4. Add tags (optional)
5. Click "Upload"

**Pro tip:** Upload everything at once by using drag-and-drop.

---

## Step 6: Process IEPs with AI

**Where:** Case → IEP Review (AI Button)

Orvared's AI can analyze IEPs to identify issues.

### How to use:
1. Upload the IEP document
2. Click "AI Review" or "Analyze IEP"
3. Wait for analysis (usually 30-60 seconds)
4. Review the analysis:
   - Compliance flags
   - Risk score
   - Missing elements
   - Recommendations
5. Accept and save to case

### What you get:
- Present levels summary
- Goal analysis
- Service minute tracking
- Compliance checklist
- Risk assessment

---

## Step 7: Track Your Time

**Where:** Use the timer in the top nav OR Case → Time Entries

Billable hours need to be tracked.

### Two ways to log:

**1. Timer (Recommended)**
- Click timer icon in top navigation
- Select case and description
- Click "Start"
- Work on case
- Click "Stop" when done
- Time is automatically saved

**2. Manual Entry**
- Go to Case → Time Entries
- Click "Add Time Entry"
- Enter date, duration, description
- Save

---

## Step 8: Create Your First Invoice

**Where:** Billing → New Invoice

When you've done work and need to bill.

### How to create:
1. Go to Billing → Invoices
2. Click "New Invoice"
3. Select Client
4. Review and add time entries
5. Add any expenses
6. Set due date and terms
7. Preview invoice
8. Send to client (or save as draft)

### Invoice includes:
- Your firm info
- Client info
- Itemized time and expenses
- Payment terms

---

## Step 9: Set Up Recurring Tasks (Optional)

**Where:** Settings → Automations

Automate reminders for important dates.

### Examples:
- IEP Annual Review (annual)
- Progress Report (quarterly)
- Re-evaluation due (every 3 years)
