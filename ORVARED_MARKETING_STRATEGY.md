### 5.2 Messaging Framework

**For All Audiences:**
- Lead with "The O That Organizes Special Education"
- Emphasize "full-stack" — it's the only one on the market
- Use IEP Scanner as lead capture/teaser

**Headline Options:**
> "The Only System That Does It All"
> "One Platform. Your Entire Practice."
> "Finally — A Real Operating System for Advocates"

### 5.3 The IEP Scanner Hook

**Strategy:** Use the IEP Scanner as the lead capture engine

**Flow:**
1. Parent/advocate visits /iep-scanner
2. Uploads IEP → gets instant risk analysis
3. Free tier shows risk level only
4. Email gate captures lead
5. Paid tier ($9.99) unlocks full analysis
6. CTA: "Need help implementing these findings? Learn about Orvared."

**Why this works:**
- No gate at entry (low friction)
- Email capture (lead gen)
- Quick income ($9.99/pop)
- Warm lead for advocacy services

### 5.4 Positioning vs. Competitors

| Competitor | Orvared Response |
|------------|------------------|
| Highlighter | "Great for document analysis, but no billing, scheduling, or case management. You'd need 3+ other tools." |
| KidvoKit | "Good for document prep, but doesn't help you run your practice." |
| Generic tools (Sheets, Dubsado) | "You're patching together a Frankenstein system. Orvared is built for your specific workflow." |
| School tools (EasyIEP, etc.) | "Those are for districts. You need your own tool." |
