## 1. What Orvared Actually Is

Orvared is a **comprehensive special education operating system** that replaces 8-10 separate apps for advocates and evaluators. It uses a **3-phase feature rollout** strategy with **ala carte pricing**.

### The Three Plans

| Plan | Monthly Price | Who It's For |
|------|---------------|--------------|
| **Advocate** | $129/mo | IEP advocates, parent advocates, special ed attorneys |
| **Evaluator** | $79/mo | Independent education specialists, psychologists |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Large firms, multi-state operations, districts |

### The Two Audiences

| User | How They Make Money | What They Charge |
|------|---------------------|------------------|
| **Advocates** | Billable hours | $60-150/hour (1,800-2,000 hrs/year) |
| **Evaluators** | Per-evaluation | $800-$3,000/evaluation (6-15/month) |

### The Math — Why $79-$129/mo Is Not Too High

| Cost Comparison | Monthly | Annual |
|-----------------|---------|--------|
| Orvared (Advocate) | $129 | $1,548 |
| Orvared (Evaluator) | $79 | $948 |
| Typical advocate's tech stack | $200-400/mo | $2,400-4,800/yr |

**Orvared pays for itself in 1-2 months** by replacing 8-10 apps (CRM, billing, calendar, documents, email, AI tools, etc.)

### Free for Parents — Viral Lead Gen

**Orvared is FREE for parents** — but they must be invited by an advocate or evaluator. This creates:
- Network effects (more advocates = more parents on platform)
- Viral growth (parents refer other parents to their advocate)
- Lead generation for you

### The IEP Scanner — Your One-Time Income Engine

Already built and positioned as:
- **Lead magnet** — free risk assessment
- **Quick income** — $9.99 for full analysis
- **Impulse buy** — low friction, filters abuse

#### Tier Structure

| Tier | Access | Price |
|------|--------|-------|
| **Free** | Risk level badge only ("HIGH RISK") | $0 |
| **Email gate** | 1-2 vague findings, no detail | Free (email capture) |
| **Paid** | Full 5-category scores, all findings, recommendations, "What This Means" section | ~$9.99 |

#### Quick Math

- 100 free scans/day → 10% email capture = 10 email leads/day
- 10% conversion on paid tier = 1 sale/day
- 1 sale × $9.99 × 30 days = **~$300/month passive income**
- Scale to 500/day = **~$1,500/month**

Plus: Each paid user is a warm lead for advocacy services.

## 10. The 3-Phase Feature Rollout Strategy

The roadmap in the code shows **Phases = Feature rollouts**, not tier hierarchies:

### Phase 1 — Available Now (Live)
**Core operational features every advocate/evaluator needs:**

1. **Case Management** — Organize students, records, and communication into one structured case
2. **Document & Timeline Organization** — Track what happened, when, and what it connects to
3. **Meeting Capture & Transcription** — Record and summarize meetings so nothing is missed
4. **Advocacy Workflow Tools** — Track actions, deadlines, and next steps across every case
5. **Evidence Tracking** — Connect documents, services, and needs into a clear case structure

### Phase 2 — Commented Out (Coming Soon)
**Advanced advocacy & case building tools:**

1. **Evidence Scoring** — Analyze strength of evidence across documents to support decision-making
2. **Case Strength Indicators** — Provide a clear view of how strong a case is based on available data
3. **Due Process Preparation Workflows** — Guide users through building a structured due process case
4. **Compensatory Services Calculator** — Estimate services owed based on missed or inadequate implementation

### Phase 3 — System-Level Intelligence
**AI-powered advanced features:**

1. **AI-Assisted Complaint Drafting** — Generate structured drafts for complaints and formal actions
2. **Parent Intake Risk Scoring** — Identify high-risk cases based on intake data
3. **Advanced IEP Goal Analytics** — Analyze goal quality and alignment to student needs
4. **District Intelligence** — Behavioral insights, patterns, playbooks for districts
5. **Evidence Builder** — Assemble and organize evidence packages for cases
